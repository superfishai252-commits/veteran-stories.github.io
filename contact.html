Help & Support

Useful Contact Information for Veterans

Whether you need healthcare, benefits assistance, or mental health support — these organizations are here for you.

🏛️

Department of Veterans Affairs

Veterans benefits, healthcare, education, and home loans.

www.va.gov

📞 1-800-827-1000

🆘

Veterans Crisis Line

Free, confidential crisis support for veterans 24/7.

📞 988 (press 1)

💬 Text to 838255

veteranscrisisline.net

🏥

VA Health Care

Apply for VA healthcare services and find a local VA facility.

www.va.gov/health

📞 1-877-222-8387

📋

VA Disability Benefits

File a claim for service-connected disability compensation.

www.benefits.va.gov

📞 1-800-827-0641

🎓

GI Bill Education Benefits

Use your education benefits for college, trade school, or on-the-job training.

www.gibill.va.gov

📞 1-888-442-4551

🏠

VA Home Loan Program

No down payment, no mortgage insurance — get a home loan.

www.va.gov/housing-assistance

📞 1-877-827-3702

🤝

Veterans Service Organizations

Groups like DAV, VFW, American Legion, and more offer free benefits assistance.

www.dav.org

www.vfw.org

📱

Military OneSource

Non-medical counseling, resources, and support for service members and families.

www.militaryonesource.mil

📞 1-800-342-9647

💙

Wounded Warrior Project

Support for post-9/11 veterans with injuries — programs, services, community.

www.woundedwarriorproject.org

📞 1-888-99-WARRIOR
